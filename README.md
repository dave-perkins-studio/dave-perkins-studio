# Dave Perkins

Senior UX designer in Bristol. Fifteen years spent on health, charity,
non-profit and public sector products, mostly asking awkward questions until the
shape of the real problem turns up. I live in Clevedon.

Before UX I spent eight years in Manchester as a creative and music lead, which
taught me more about people and persuasion than any design course managed. I
still play, usually in a baritone tuning, usually too loud.

I'm currently on contract with [SystemSeed](https://systemseed.com), doing
research, flows, prototypes and accessibility for
[Concern Worldwide](https://systemseed.com/clients/fighting-extreme-poverty-with-concern-worldwide/),
[Globethics](https://systemseed.com/clients/ethics-made-accessible-redesigning-globethics-digital-platform/),
[UKRI Innovate UK](https://systemseed.com/clients/ukri-innovate-uk/) and the
[Kappa Delta Foundation](https://systemseed.com/clients/kappa-delta/), along
with Cycling UK and the Sustainable Health Index. None of that work lives here,
so this page is closer to an index than a portfolio.

What is here is the other half: side projects, small enough to actually finish,
built to keep my hands in the making end of the job.
